About Sorella Home Solutions

Focusing on what truly matters...

Sorella Home Solutions is a women-owned home services company dedicated to providing exceptional property, project, and home management solutions. We deliver peace of mind through personalized service, high attention to detail, and technology-driven communication to maximize efficiencies. Our mission is to be the trusted partner that prioritizes our clients’ experience, handling every detail with care and efficiency..

Reclaim Your Most Valuable Asset

Two decades of expertise, one mission: giving you back your time

Founded by Megan Calabrese, a seasoned leader with over 20 years of experience in real estate management, Sorella was built on a foundation of expertise, trust, and client-first service.

Throughout her career, Megan has overseen millions of square feet of real estate, from medical and office properties to residential communities. She has led teams, managed complex operations, and delivered best-in-class service for clients across the Northeast and Florida. With Sorella, she brings that same level of professionalism and precision to the residential space—assembling a team of industry experts to provide comprehensive solutions for discerning, time-conscious homeowners.

Sorella Home Solutions offers a comprehensive suite of home management services, designed to support busy professionals, families, and homeowners. We provide property management, project management and oversight, move management, and concierge-level services. As a women-owned business, we bring a unique, client-centered approach in an industry that is often fragmented.

Meet Leigh K. Roy

Director of Operations

As Sorella's Director of Operations, Leigh ensures every detail behind the scenes runs seamlessly — so our clients can enjoy peace of mind knowing their homes are in expert hands. With more than 20 years of experience in real estate operations, property management, and project coordination, she blends professionalism with genuine care in everything she does.

Before joining Sorella, Leigh held senior roles with leading firms including FoxRock Properties, Pembroke Real Estate, and Fidelity Investments' Real Estate Company. Her career has been defined by her ability to streamline systems, enhance communication, and deliver exceptional client experiences.

A lifelong South Shore resident, Leigh is as passionate about community as she is about operations. She's volunteered with organizations such as Sibling Connections and Hingham Girls Softball and brings that same sense of integrity and service to her work every day.
